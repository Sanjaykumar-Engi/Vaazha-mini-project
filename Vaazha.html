Idly

Idli is a soft, fluffy, and savory steamed cake originating from South India. Made from a naturally fermented batter of soaked rice and lentils, it is one of the world's healthiest, vegan, and gluten-free breakfast staples. It is traditionally paired with coconut chutney and sambar.

Dosa

Dosa is a thin, crispy, and savory pancake originating from South India. Made from a fermented batter of rice and urad dal (black gram), it is a popular breakfast and snack item. Dosas are typically served with coconut chutney and sambar, making for a delicious and satisfying meal.

Rice

Basmati rice is a premium variety of long, slender-grained aromatic rice traditionally grown in the Himalayan foothills of the Indian subcontinent. Derived from the Sanskrit word vasmati, meaning "fragrant," it is globally celebrated for its distinctive nutty aroma, which is often compared to popcorn .

Pongal

Pongal is a traditional South Indian dish made from rice and moong dal (yellow lentils). It is typically seasoned with cumin, black pepper, ginger, and curry leaves, giving it a savory and aromatic flavor. Pongal is often enjoyed as a comforting breakfast or festive meal, especially during the harvest festival.
